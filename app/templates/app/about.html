Small, independently-owned restaurants are what keeps Seattle a foodie's paradise.

No public gatherings means that it's tougher to visit our favourite neighbourhood haunts. However, a lot of them are still open for takeout and delivery.

So I made a little thing. Doordash, Grubhub, and Yelp still work of course, but the sorting algorithms seem to favor national chains.

Just submit a restaurant with as much info as you know and it'll be up on the list!
